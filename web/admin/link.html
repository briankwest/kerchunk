About

mod_link bridges this repeater to a central reflector running kerchunk-reflectd. Local RX audio is encoded as Opus (24 kHz, 32 kbps, FEC) and sent over SRTP/UDP. Incoming reflector audio is decoded, fed to the local playback path, and the radio is keyed via the link tail timer.

DTMF *73<n># switches talkgroup at runtime (e.g. *731# for TG 1).
